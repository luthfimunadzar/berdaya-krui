Sustainable &
Responsible Resorts

A responsible resort run by Berdaya Hospitality management, opening soon in Krui end of 2020. Nature lovers and adventurer tourists are called to stay in our quiet and breathtaking spot in southern Sumatra.

Berdaya Resort is a responsible, bungalow setting village that is completely surrounded by Sumatra's virgin nature. Enjoy surfing, biking, hiking, visiting local communities while exploring the breathtaking, untouched beaches and Sumatran wilderness.

Berdaya Surf Academy

Developing local surfing talent in Krui of South Sumatra is one of our earliest movement. Through Berdaya Surf Academy, visitors and surfers from around the world can share their knowledge on basic surfing skills to children and local communities in Krui, contributing directly to Krui Local youth empowerment.

Our Activity

Sustainable & Responsible Resorts
A responsible resort run by Berdaya hospitality management. Giving socioeconomic impacts for local community, resort workers, and visitors.

How We Do

01.

For Visitors: Experience living genuinely and create lasting memories. Connect with the locals. Surf responsibly. Teach English. Teach the local about conservations. Connect with communities and local entrepreneurs. All-inclusive while staying in our resort through Berdaya Purposeful Programs ©. Kids are allowed too.

02.

For Community: Our resorts are proudly made and established by the power of local communities & local sustainable materials. We knit close partnerships with Local Leader of Communities. Build our resorts in Tanah Desa with fair trade mechanism. Conduct Berdaya Market while showcasing selected UMKMs. We pick & showcase selected arts & performances in our communal space. We connect community and visitors.

03.

For Workers: We do inclusive welfare programs for workers. Above-UMR Salaries, BPJS guaranteed, fair working hours, intensive & continuous learnings and hospitality classes for employees.

Surf Academy for Local Youth Empowerment
Developing local talents surfing talent in Krui is one of our earliest movement that we believed will impact directly for the youth communities in Krui. Through our academy surfers from around the world can contribute directly for the local youth by giving them a surfing education and impact directly.

How We Do

01.

Vocational Course: Short course for local community to establish their tourism awareness knowledge and what they can do to involve in surf Industry.

02.

Mentoring: Incubation for local talent who join Berdaya Program and measure their skill development to become local here or surf industry role model in their area.

03.

Giving Jobs Opportunity: Developing local talents skill and educated them with good working ethos. We give recommendation and promote Berdaya Team to fulfill the need of job requirement in our Area.

Berdaya Purposeful Programs
We conduct journey enriching-activities while at the same time, impacting lives to those in needs.

How We Do

01.

Education: Monthly English classes to our partner elementary schools, teaching basic conversational English & knowledge on tourism potential within their local hometown.

02.

Environment: Monthly programs together with local communities, from Beach Clean-ups Walk, to Marine Conservation Class, and Environmental Awareness Course.

03.

Local Business: Supporting Local and Aspiring Entrepreneurs, teaching business & financial skills, introducing to networking and distribution channels. Creating local entrepreneurs, making quality jobs for durable economic growth, reducing poverty.
